Advanced UX Design

User Experience is a combination of tasks focused on optimization of a product for effective and enjoyable use. User Experience Design is the process of development and improvement of quality interaction between a user and all facets of a company. User Experience Design is responsible for being hands on with the process of research, testing, development, content, and prototyping to test for quality results. User Experience Design is in theory a non-digital (cognitive science) practice, but used and defined predominantly by digital industries.
