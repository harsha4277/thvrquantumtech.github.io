Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing THVR QUANTUMTECH. We strive to ensure your satisfaction with every product and service you receive from us. However, we understand that situations may arise where returns and refunds become necessary. This policy outlines our guidelines for returns and refund requests.

Refund Eligibility

Software and Services: Due to the nature of digital products and services, we generally do not offer refunds once the product or service has been delivered or accessed. Exceptions may apply in cases of technical issues or non-delivery.

Returns

Physical Products: If you have received a physical product from us and it is damaged or defective, please contact our customer support within [number of days] days of receiving the product. We will guide you through the return process.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at support@thvrquantumtech.com within 15 days of your purchase. Please provide your order number and a detailed explanation of the reason for your refund request.

Processing Refunds

Once your refund request is received and approved, we will initiate the refund process. Please allow 15-20 days for the refund to be processed and credited back to your original payment method.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Return & Refund Policy, please contact us at support@thvrquantumtech.com. We are here to assist you and ensure your experience with THVR QUANTUMTECH is positive and hassle-free.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Return & Refund Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be noted at the top of the policy.
